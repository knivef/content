## Formatting Notes

- The `---` horizontal rules between sections are mandatory — they create clean visual separation in email clients
- The blockquote `>` tagline in the header renders as a styled callout in Substack/Beehiiv
- The `**bold**` text on company names and figures renders consistently across all email clients
- Inline `[text](url)` links are universally compatible — never use reference-style links
- The footer italics `*text*` render as a soft grey in most email clients — ideal for footer content
