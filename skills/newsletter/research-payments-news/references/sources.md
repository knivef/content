## 📊 Stats & Data Sources (for "By the Numbers")

- **Stablecoin supply:** DefiLlama (defillama.com), The Block Data (data.theblock.co)
- **Payment volumes:** BIS reports, Fed payments study, ECB payment statistics
- **Real-time payment stats:** FPC (US RTP data), FedNow dashboard, NPCI (India UPI stats)
- **VC funding totals:** CB Insights, PitchBook, KPMG Pulse of Fintech
- **Crypto payment adoption:** Chainalysis reports, Visa/MC quarterly reports
